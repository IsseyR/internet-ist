The Internet

What is it?


The internet is a system of interconnected computers around the globe that uses the global protocol suit (TCP/IP) to link devices worldwide. The networks that make up the internet consist of private, public, accedemic, business and Government networks of local to a global scope.


What is its purpose?


The purpose of the internet can cahnge with the user and as technology evolves and advances over time. The internet in its current form has a purpose of sharing data internet across the world, it provides access to billions of people all over the world and connects us all together.
