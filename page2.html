Information

How to access it?


The internet can be accessed through Internet Service providers (ISP's). These companies often charge a fee for connectivity. A wide range of services are often available usally Differentiated by the data transfer speed offered.


How to use it?


The internet can be used through an Internet Browser, commonly included in most computers. These Internet browsers often has a choice between serach engines, such as google or bing. search engines crawl the internet indexing websites that are than made publicly available to click on in a search query.
